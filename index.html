About Our Alliance

Supporting FIRST Robotics teams in the Sun Prairie area through community-powered STEM education.

Prairie Robotics Alliance, Inc. is a Wisconsin 501(c)(3) nonprofit booster organization dedicated to advancing STEM education through the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) program.

We support FRC Team 10522, providing students with hands-on experience in engineering, programming, project management, and teamwork — skills that translate directly into high-demand careers in technology and manufacturing.

Our team operates out of Prairie Phoenix Academy in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin. We are currently seeking to expand our facilities into a full-scale prototype fabrication lab with state-of-the-art equipment including CNC machining, 3D printing, and laser cutting.

FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global robotics community that prepares young people for the future. Learn more at firstinspires.org

What Students Gain

  • Mechanical & electrical engineering fundamentals
  • CAD/CAM design and CNC fabrication
  • Programming and autonomous systems
  • Project management and teamwork
  • Public speaking and fundraising
  • Direct connections to local industry
  • Scholarship and career opportunities

Meet Team 10522

The students, mentors, and board members who make it all happen.

FRC Team 10522 — "Team Scrappy" — is a community of students, mentors, and supporters in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin, united by a passion for robotics, engineering, and making things work. Together we design, build, and compete.

Where Your Dollars Go

🤖

Robot Build & Parts

The Future: Fabrication Lab Coming Soon

We're building a state-of-the-art fabrication and prototyping lab — a permanent, professional-grade workspace where students learn real manufacturing skills used in industry every day.

This dedicated space will serve as a workforce pipeline, giving students direct experience with the tools and processes used by local employers in engineering and manufacturing.

  • CNC Machining
  • Laser Cutting
  • 3D Printing
  • CAD/CAM Workstations
  • Electronics Lab
  • Safety Training
